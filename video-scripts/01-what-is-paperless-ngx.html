Video 1: What Is Paperless NGX?

Target: 2-3 minutes Type: Concept (standalone)

Before You Record

  • Paperless NGX running at localhost:8000 (or your server)
  • Test data loaded (run test-data/upload.py) — 100 documents including soil tests, insurance policies, equipment records, leases, etc.
  • Browser at the Paperless dashboard, logged in
  • Close any tabs/notifications that might pop up during recording
Hook (0:00 - 0:20)
On Screen Narration
Show Paperless dashboard with documents visible. Slowly scroll through the document list. "If you're anything like me, you've got years of paperwork sitting in filing cabinets, desk drawers, maybe scattered across a few folders on your computer. Insurance policies, equipment records, leases, service invoices — it adds up fast."
What It Is (0:20 - 1:00)
On Screen Narration
Show Paperless dashboard — the main document list view with thumbnails. "Paperless NGX is a free, open-source app that turns all of that paper into a searchable digital archive. You scan a document or drop in a PDF, and it does the rest."
Click Click on a document to open it. Pick something with visible OCR text — like a soil test report or an equipment service record. "It reads the text off the page automatically — that's called OCR, optical character recognition — so even a photo of a handwritten note becomes searchable text."
Key Features Tour (1:00 - 2:00)
On Screen Narration
Click Go back to dashboard. Click the search bar and type a keyword like "nitrogen" or "John Deere." Show results appearing. "You can search by keyword and find any document that mentions it. Need to find every document that references nitrogen? Just type it in."
Click Show the tags sidebar or filter by a tag. Show a tag like "corn" or "insurance" or "herbicide." "You can organize everything with tags and document types. Tag something as 'insurance' or 'herbicide' and filter your whole archive in one click."
Click Open a document and show the metadata panel — date, tags, correspondent, document type. "Every document gets a date, tags, who it's from, what type it is. So when you need to find a lease agreement from two years ago or pull up all your insurance policies, you just filter and there they are."
Why It Matters (2:00 - 2:30)
On Screen Narration
Show Back at the dashboard showing the full document list. Maybe slowly scroll to show the volume of documents. "The real value is that it runs on your own hardware. Your documents stay yours — they're not sitting on somebody else's server. And it's completely free. No subscription, no per-page fees."
Wrap-Up (2:30 - 2:45)
On Screen Narration
Show Dashboard view. Let it sit for a moment. "That's Paperless NGX in a nutshell — scan it, search it, find it. In the next video I'll show you what we added on top of it to make searching even smarter."
